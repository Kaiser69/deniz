My Adventures
with my Dad

2024 summer(Jun/July), my dad and I are embarking on an exhilarating adventure through Malaysia and Australia. In Malaysia, we’ll explore Borneo's rainforests, enjoy Kuala Lumpur’s vibrant city life, and relax on Langkawi’s stunning beaches. Our journey continues in Australia, where we'll snorkel in the Great Barrier Reef, take in Sydney’s iconic sights, and experience the Outback’s rugged beauty and Aboriginal culture. It’s set to be a memorable trip, filled with breathtaking landscapes, thrilling activities, and unforgettable moments together.
