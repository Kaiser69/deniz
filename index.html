My Adventures
with my Dad

Summer 2024 (June/July)

For my 10th birthday, my dad gifted me the most incredible adventure, and we’re setting off this summer on an exhilarating journey through Malaysia and Australia. In Malaysia, we’ll trek through Borneo’s lush rainforests, soak up the vibrant energy of Kuala Lumpur, and unwind on Langkawi’s stunning beaches.

Our journey then takes us to Australia, where we’ll snorkel in the Great Barrier Reef, explore Melbourne’s cultural scene, and take a scenic drive along the Great Ocean Road, famous for its breathtaking sandstone cliffs and the iconic Twelve Apostles. We’ll also experience Sydney’s iconic landmarks and dive into Adelaide’s unique charm. Finally, we’ll venture into the Outback to discover its rugged beauty and rich Aboriginal culture.

It’s shaping up to be an unforgettable trip—filled with breathtaking landscapes, exciting experiences, and meaningful moments together.
